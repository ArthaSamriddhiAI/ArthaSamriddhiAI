The Three-Layer Mental Model

Any system that allows agents to act under uncertainty must separate three questions without collapsing them:

E

Evidence Layer

"What does the system believe about the world?" 10 analysis agents (Fundamental, Technical, Sectoral, Macro, Sentiment, Financial Risk, Industry and Business Model, Behavioural and Historical, Unlisted Equity, PMS/AIF) produce structured, framework-grounded assessments. A Master Synthesis Agent acts as investment committee chair. Produces beliefs, not decisions.

G

Governance Layer

"What actions are permitted right now?" The control plane. 3 governance agents (Allocation, Risk Interpretation, Review) assess proposals against mandates, concentration limits, and investor constraints. A deterministic rule engine evaluates hard and soft constraints. Real-time permissioning, not retrospective compliance.

A

Accountability Layer

"Who acted, under what authority, and how is it recorded?" The Audit Explorer provides system-wide and portfolio-level traceability. Every action, every edit, every freeze/unfreeze, every governance decision is recorded with actor, timestamp, evidence, and reasoning. Every action in this system is recorded.

Full Decision Pipeline

Intent→ Evidence Freeze→ 10 Analysis Agents→ Master Synthesis→ 3 Governance Agents→ Rule Engine→ Permission Filter→ Decision Record

The orchestrator has bounded authority: cannot place trades, cannot override rules, cannot invent objectives.

Design Principles

Agents surface risk; they do NOT decide

Decision authority is always explicit and human.

Synthesis is not a decision

Aggregation layers clarify the landscape; they don't remove responsibility.

Governance is real-time permissioning

Not retrospective compliance. Deterministic, versioned, executable.

Decision telemetry over model telemetry

The system records human judgment and intent, not AI chain-of-thought.

Learning is governed and explicit

No silent drift. Judgment evolves like policy: slowly, versioned, with traceability.

Off-system judgment is a feature

Humans may incorporate experience and discretion no model can encode.

Multi-Lens Analysis Layer

Before governance agents assess risk, 10 analysis agents independently evaluate the proposed action through different analytical lenses. A Master Synthesis Agent aggregates their findings into a unified committee position.
