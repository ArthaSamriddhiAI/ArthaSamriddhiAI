# ArthaSamriddhiAI — 5 Minute Demo Video Script
## "The Rs 87 Crore Question"

**Duration**: 5 minutes
**Format**: Screen recording + AI voiceover (no face)
**Music**: Dark ambient opening → builds to cinematic mid → resolves warm
**Mood**: Tension → Revelation → Trust → Awe

---

## THE SCRIPT

### [0:00 – 0:30] COLD OPEN — The Crisis

**Screen**: Black. Single white cursor blinking.

Then type, letter by letter (typewriter effect):

```
Your AI just recommended selling Rs 2 crore of equities
from a conservative retiree's portfolio.

The client calls.

"Why?"
```

**[2-second pause. Black.]**

```
You don't know.
Because your AI doesn't know.
Because it was never designed to answer that question.
```

**Music**: Low, ominous drone begins.

---

### [0:30 – 1:00] THE PROMISE

**Screen**: Landing page hero fades in. Slow, cinematic.

**Voiceover** (calm, measured):

> *"What if every AI decision came with a receipt?"*
>
> *"Not a log. Not a timestamp. A complete, traceable chain — from what the system believed, to who assessed the risk, to why the decision was made."*

**Screen**: The three words pulse: **Evidence. Governance. Accountability.**

> *"This is ArthaSamriddhiAI. The Portfolio Operating System."*

**Screen**: Click "Enter the Platform". Dashboard appears.

---

### [1:00 – 1:45] THE COMMAND CENTER

**Screen**: Dashboard loads — Rs 87.3 Cr AUM, 6 clients, drift warnings.

**Voiceover**:

> *"87 crores. Six families. Three need your attention right now."*

**Screen**: Pointer hovers over "Drift 33%" on Sharma Family Office.

> *"The Sharma Family Office. 50 crores. 48 holdings across 9 asset classes. And their portfolio has drifted 33 points from target."*

**Screen**: Click Sharma → Portfolio loads. Allocation bar, holdings.

> *"Equities down to 3%. Mutual funds at 46%. Real estate heavy at 33%. This isn't what was agreed."*

**Screen**: Click "Rebalance" tab. Drift table appears.

> *"The system sees the drift. But here's what's different — it doesn't act. It asks."*

---

### [1:45 – 3:15] THE GOVERNANCE MOMENT — This is the WOW

**Screen**: Click "Run Governance Review" button. Intent form appears with Sharma pre-selected, holdings auto-populated.

**Voiceover**:

> *"One click. The system already knows every holding. Every share. Every unit."*

**Screen**: Click "Submit Intent". Show the loading spinner. **Wait the full 15-20 seconds** — don't cut. Let the tension build.

**Voiceover** (over the wait):

> *"Right now, three things are happening simultaneously.*
>
> *First — the system freezes the evidence. Every price, every NAV, every risk score — locked at this exact moment. Immutable. If anyone asks in three years what the system believed when this decision was made, the answer is preserved.*
>
> *Second — three independent AI agents are analyzing this portfolio. They cannot see each other's reasoning. Each has its own mandate. Each produces a structured opinion. They surface risk. They do not decide.*
>
> *Third — deterministic governance rules are being loaded. Not AI. Not opinions. Rules. Written by humans. Enforced by code. No exceptions."*

**Screen**: Result appears — "Rejected". 3 agents, 100+ rules evaluated.

> *"The verdict: Rejected."*

**Screen**: Click "View Full Analysis". Agent cards appear — all three showing "high" risk.

> *"Three agents. Three independent assessments. All flagging high risk. But look —"*

**Screen**: Zoom into the confidence levels: 92%, 95%, 90%.

> *"92% confidence. 95%. 90%. These aren't guesses. These are structured, bounded assessments from AI agents that cannot override rules, cannot place trades, cannot invent objectives."*

**Screen**: Switch to Rules tab. Show the red X marks on failed rules.

> *"And here's where governance speaks. Max single position: violated. Portfolio risk threshold: violated. The rules don't negotiate. The rules don't have bad days."*

---

### [3:15 – 4:15] THE TRAIL — The Awe Moment

**Screen**: Switch to "Trace DAG" tab. The timeline unfolds.

**Voiceover** (slower, more weight):

> *"Now. This is the part that changes everything."*

**Screen**: Slowly scroll through the trace nodes: Intent Received → Evidence Frozen → Agent Invoked → Agent Output → Rule Evaluated → Permission Denied.

> *"Every. Single. Step. Recorded as a node in a causal graph. Click any one."*

**Screen**: Click "View Data" on the Evidence Frozen node. Data expands.

> *"Here is what the system believed. Frozen at the moment of decision."*

**Screen**: Click Agent Output node.

> *"Here is what the agent said. Why it said it. What it flagged."*

**Screen**: Click Permission Denied node.

> *"Here is why the decision was blocked. The exact rule. The exact value. The exact threshold."*

**Screen**: Navigate to Telemetry screen. Show 130+ nodes, disagreement detection.

> *"130 trace nodes. 32 decisions. Every judgment preserved. Every disagreement surfaced."*

**[Beat.]**

> *"This isn't a log. This is institutional memory."*

---

### [4:15 – 4:45] THE MANDATE

**Screen**: Navigate to Sharma's investor profile. Show mandates section.

**Voiceover**:

> *"And the rules aren't one-size-fits-all. Each client has their own governance mandates."*

**Screen**: Show existing mandates — "Max Single Position 10%", "Excluded Sectors: Coal, Tobacco", "Require Committee Approval: Yes".

> *"The Sharma family has a 10% position cap. Coal and tobacco are excluded. Every rebalance requires committee sign-off. These aren't suggestions. They're law — enforced by the system, every time, without exception."*

---

### [4:45 – 5:00] THE CLOSE

**Screen**: Fade to landing page. Slow scroll to the final section.

**Voiceover** (final, conviction):

> *"In regulated finance, intelligence without governance is not progress."*

**[Pause.]**

> *"It is a liability."*

**Screen**: Fade to black. Logo appears.

> *"Models are replaceable. Governance is not."*

**Screen**:

```
ArthaSamriddhiAI
Evidence. Governance. Accountability.
```

**Music**: Resolves. Silence.

### Voiceover

- **Tool**: ElevenLabs (voice: "Daniel" — British, authoritative)
- **Record narration first**, then sync screen recording to match
- **Pace**: Deliberate. The script has built-in pauses. Use them.
- **Key moments to land**: "They surface risk. They do not decide." / "The rules don't negotiate." / "This is institutional memory."

### Music

- **0:00-0:30**: Dark ambient drone (search: "dark cinematic intro")
- **0:30-1:45**: Build slowly — soft piano or strings
- **1:45-3:15**: Tension builds — pulsing bass, subtle percussion
- **3:15-4:15**: Peak — full cinematic swell when trace unfolds
- **4:15-5:00**: Resolve — warm, confident, minimal

### Post-Production

1. **Text overlays** at key moments:
   - 0:00: Typewriter text on black
   - 2:15: "3 agents. 0 decision authority." (lower third)
   - 3:15: "Every step. A causal graph." (lower third)
   - 4:50: Logo + tagline

2. **Zoom effects**: Subtle 2-5% slow zoom on data-heavy screens to create movement

3. **Transitions**: Cross-dissolve only. No wipes. No flash.

4. **Color**: Slightly desaturated. Cool tone. Professional, not flashy.

### Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Browser: Chrome, full screen, no bookmarks bar, no extensions visible
- [ ] Resolution: 1920x1080 or 2560x1440
- [ ] Sharma FO has mandates set (excluded sectors, max position, committee approval)
- [ ] At least 8 decisions with trace nodes (done — 130 nodes)
- [ ] Dashboard shows all 6 clients with AUM and drift
- [ ] Market brief loads (may need to wait 10s after page load)
- [ ] Clean mouse cursor (no jitter)
- [ ] No notifications/popups on screen

## WHY THIS SCRIPT WORKS

**The hook** (0:00): Starts with a problem EVERY wealth manager has experienced — an unexplainable AI recommendation. Immediate emotional connection.

**The build** (1:00-1:45): Shows real money (Rs 87 Cr), real clients, real drift. This isn't a demo — this feels like a real wealth management desk.

**The wow** (1:45-3:15): The 15-second wait during governance intent is NOT a bug — it's a FEATURE. You narrate what's happening inside while the viewer watches. When the result appears with 3 agents and 100+ rules, the viewer understands the depth.

**The awe** (3:15-4:15): The trace DAG is the moment that differentiates this from every other AI tool. "This isn't a log. This is institutional memory." — that line lands because the viewer just SAW the evidence.

**The anchor** (4:45-5:00): "Models are replaceable. Governance is not." — leaves the viewer with a conviction, not a feature list.
