## DETAILED SCRIPT

**Screen**: Black screen with white text fading in, one line at a time.

>
> *"The client asks: Why?"*
>
> *"The regulator asks: Who decided this?"*
>
> *"Your compliance officer asks: What evidence was used?"*
>
> *"And your AI has no answer. Because it wasn't designed to have one."*

**Screen**: Fade to brief montage of generic AI chatbot interfaces, then a red "?" overlay.

**Voiceover**:
*"Most AI in finance today is designed to predict. To recommend. To optimize. But it's not designed to be questioned. Not designed to explain. Not designed to be governed. And in regulated finance, an AI that cannot explain itself... is a liability."*

### [0:45 - 1:30] THE QUESTION

**Screen**: Fade to the landing page hero section of ArthaSamriddhiAI. Slow scroll down.

**Voiceover**:
*"The question isn't whether AI can manage portfolios. The question is: how do you design AI systems that support probabilistic judgment, remain auditable, preserve human accountability, and improve over time — without drifting outside governance?"*

**Screen**: The "Evidence - Governance - Accountability" pill badge on the landing page glows.

*"This is the question that ArthaSamriddhiAI was built to answer."*

**Screen**: Scroll to the EGA three-layer diagram on the landing page. Each layer lights up as it's mentioned:

*"Three layers. One invariant. Evidence produces beliefs, not decisions. Governance determines what's permitted. Accountability records who acted, under what authority."*

---

### [1:30 - 2:00] INTRODUCING EGA

**Screen**: Scroll to the "Design Principles" section. Highlight the first card.

**Voiceover**:
*"Let me show you what this looks like in practice. Not theory — actual client data, actual AI agents, actual governance rules. Running right now."*

**Screen**: Click "Enter the Platform" button. Transition to the app.

### [2:00 - 4:30] ACT 1: THE CLIENT (Evidence Layer)

**Screen**: Advisor Dashboard loads. Show the AUM figure, 6 clients, AI Market Brief.

**Voiceover**:
*"This is the advisor's command center. Six clients. Combined AUM of over 50 crores. Each with a different risk profile. Each with a different portfolio. The system knows all of them."*

**Screen**: Click on "Sharma Family Office" in the client table. Portfolio screen loads.

*"The Sharma Family Office. 25 crores across 48 holdings. Equities, mutual funds, real estate, PMS, AIF, gold, insurance. A complex, multi-generational portfolio."*

**Screen**: Show the summary cards (Total Invested, Current Value, Gain/Loss). Show the allocation bar — multi-colored with 9 asset classes.

*"Every holding is valued in real-time using actual market data. 11 million mutual fund NAV records. A million stock prices. Commodity and forex rates. All flowing through automated daily pipelines."*

**Screen**: Briefly show the Evidence Explorer — click "Stocks", show the Nifty 500 data table. Then "Mutual Funds" showing 7,748 schemes.

*"This is the Evidence layer. What does the system believe about the world? It believes what the data tells it. No more. No less. Evidence produces beliefs — never decisions."*

---

### [4:30 - 6:30] ACT 2: THE COMMITTEE (Governance Layer)

**Screen**: Go back to Sharma Family Office portfolio. Click "Rebalance" tab. Show the drift analysis.

**Voiceover**:
*"The system detects drift. Sharma's mutual fund allocation is 12 points above target. Their equity exposure has drifted. The system doesn't fix this automatically. It asks permission."*

**Screen**: Click the "Performance" tab. Show period returns, benchmark comparison, top/bottom performers.

*"Before any action, agents are consulted. Not one AI — three independent AI agents. Each with a different mandate."*

**Screen**: Navigate to New Intent. Fill in: Rebalance, Sharma's symbols, holdings. Submit.

*"Watch what happens. The system freezes the evidence at the moment of decision. Then three agents — independently, without seeing each other's reasoning — analyze the portfolio."*

**Screen**: The result appears. Click "View Full Analysis". Switch to Agents tab.

*"The Allocation Agent sees concentration risk. Confidence: 92%. The Risk Interpretation Agent flags high volatility exposure. Confidence: 95%. The Review Agent synthesizes — and notes where they disagree."*

**Screen**: Zoom into the agent cards. Highlight the risk levels, confidence bars, drivers, flags.

*"This is not a black box giving you an answer. This is a digital risk committee. Each voice heard. Each opinion structured. Each flag preserved."*

**Screen**: Switch to Rules tab. Show the rule evaluation table — green checks and red X marks.

*"Now the governance layer speaks. Deterministic rules. Not AI. Not opinions. Rules. Written in YAML, version-controlled, sandboxed. If a hard rule is violated, the action is blocked. No exceptions. No override — unless a human explicitly approves."*

*"The max single position rule: violated. The portfolio risk threshold: violated. The family office committee approval rule: triggered."*

**Screen**: Show Permission tab with "Rejected" status and specific rejection reasons.

*"The system's verdict: rejected. Not because an AI decided to reject. Because governance rules — written by humans, enforced deterministically — determined that this action is not permitted right now."*

**Pause. Beat.**

*"This is the key insight. Agents surface risk. They do not decide. Synthesis is not decision. Governance is real-time permissioning, not retrospective compliance."*

### [6:30 - 8:00] ACT 3: THE TRAIL (Accountability Layer)

**Screen**: Switch to Trace DAG tab. Show the vertical timeline with colored nodes.

**Voiceover**:
*"Now comes the part that changes everything. The accountability layer."*

*"Every step of this decision is recorded as a node in a causal graph. Not a log. Not a timestamp. A directed acyclic graph where every outcome can be traced back to its cause."*

**Screen**: Click to expand "Intent Received" node — show the data payload.

*"Here is the intent. What was requested, by whom, with what parameters."*

**Screen**: Expand "Evidence Frozen" node.

*"Here is the evidence snapshot. What the system believed at the moment of decision. Frozen. Immutable. If you audit this decision three years from now, you see exactly the same evidence."*

**Screen**: Expand "Agent Output" nodes — show all three.

*"Here is what each agent said. Their risk level. Their confidence. Their drivers. Their flags. Preserved as they were — no chain-of-thought stored, only decision artifacts and rationale."*

**Screen**: Expand "Rule Evaluated" nodes.

*"Here is every rule that was checked. The condition. The context. Whether it passed or failed. The exact values at evaluation time."*

**Screen**: Expand "Permission Denied" node.

*"And here is the final outcome. With the precise reasons. Traceable. Reconstructable. Defensible."*

**Screen**: Navigate to Telemetry screen. Show the full dashboard.

*"The Decision Telemetry Bus captures everything. Not model internals — decision artifacts. 130 trace nodes across 8 decisions. Agent disagreements detected and surfaced. Approval rates tracked. Every judgment preserved."*

## EXECUTION PLAN

### Step 1: Screen Recording
- Use OBS Studio or similar (free, 1080p/4K)
- Record each screen segment separately (easier to re-record)
- Browser: Chrome full-screen, no bookmarks bar, no extensions visible
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Mouse movements: smooth and deliberate, not rushed
- Pause 1-2 seconds on each important data point

### Step 2: AI Voiceover
- Use ElevenLabs or similar for professional narration
- Voice: Male, British/Indian accent, authoritative but warm
- Pace: Deliberate. Not rushed. Let the words land.
- Record narration first, then time screen recordings to match

### Step 3: Editing
- Tool: DaVinci Resolve (free) or CapCut
- Add text overlays for key principles (the "Agents surface risk" moment)
- Add subtle transitions between screens (cross-dissolve, not flashy)
- Add music bed (quiet, ambient — increases slightly at emotional peaks)
- Add lower-third annotations: "Evidence Layer", "Governance Layer", "Accountability Layer"
- Color grade: slightly desaturated, professional tone

### Step 4: Title Cards
- Opening: Black + white text, fade in line by line
- Closing: Logo + "Evidence. Governance. Accountability."
- Closing card: Website URL, contact (if applicable)

### Step 5: Thumbnail
- Dark navy background
- Gold accent line
- Text: "Why Your AI Needs Governance" or "The Portfolio Operating System"
- Professional, Bloomberg-esque aesthetic
